About Us

வள்ளி அம்பாள்
Metal Crafts

❧

With over 55 years of heritage craftsmanship, Valliambal Metal Crafts is your trusted source for authentic handcrafted metal religious articles, idols, and pooja items. Founded under the legacy of Shanmuga Vilas, we have proudly served temples and households across Tamil Nadu.

Located in Hosur, we supply all essential pooja articles, decorative lamps, idols, and temple accessories needed for homes and temples — crafted from the finest metals with traditional South Indian artisan skill.

✦ Blessed by ✦
Sri Sengamuneeswarar Vinayagar
Sri Santhana Patrakaliamman
Sri Chandirashoodeswarar

We specialise in articles for temples in Kumbakonam, Nachiyarkovil, and Swamimalai — the heart of South Indian bronze craftsmanship.

What We Offer

Services சேவைகள் & Specialities

❧

We provide all pooja articles, decorative lamps, brass vessels, and temple accessories required for temples and homes — including Thiruvachi, Kavasam, Kodimaram, Vaganam and custom fabricated items. All orders are crafted and delivered as per your exact specifications.

Every piece that leaves our workshop is inspected for finish, proportion, and detail — because we believe sacred items deserve nothing less than perfection. Customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do.
