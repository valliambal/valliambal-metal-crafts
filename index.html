Metal Crafts

❧

Valliambal Metal Crafts brings you the finest handcrafted metal religious idols and artifacts from Tamil Nadu. Every piece is shaped with meticulous attention, honoring centuries-old traditions of South Indian metal craft.

Whether you seek a sacred idol for your home shrine, a meaningful gift, or a custom commission, our artisan Selvam and his team guide you with patience and expertise.

We offer full customisation — tell us your vision and we will bring it to life in bronze, brass, or copper.
